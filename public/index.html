Play

Shoot the stars out of heaven and uncover a special christmas present!

The tree can be moved sidewards with the left and right arrow keys. It shoots with the spacebar.

If nothing happens try to click on the card. When you see a green shadow around the christmas card you're ready to play.

Enjoy!
